Vision:

At Metrochem, we believe that we have a role to play in enriching lives through increased access to affordable healthcare. We believe that everything we do, every single decision, every single product, every single process, every one of our moves impact the world healthcare landscape in some way, shape or form, and we strive for that to be a positive impact. Our beliefs have instilled in us a sense of accountability. We are accountable to our customers, our partners, our employees, our families, and the society at large. We are accountable to produce the highest quality products, we are accountable to produce affordable medications, we are accountable for timely delivery of products, we are accountable to enhance the development of our employees, and we are accountable to give back to the community.

As a purpose driven organization, we envision to constantly push our boundaries and challenge, not only us but also our competition to be more innovative, more efficient, more responsible and more committed to the cause of healthcare accessibility. Be it intermediates, APIs, pellets or FDFs, we aim to lead by example in whatever portfolio we pick and we aim to set standards.

We pride ourselves in exceeding expectations. Our purpose and vision helped shape a culture driven by Innovation, Integrity, Customer Focus and Safety. These four pillars serve as a guidance for every employee at Metrochem.

A deep dive into our values:

Innovation:

Innovation gives an organization its competitive edge. Here at Metrochem we focus on innovation across people, process, products and technology, through Knowledge sharing and cross functional collaboration.

Focus:

We respect the trust our stakeholders invested in us, and we strive to that the community has put in us to develop products that safe-guard the health, we have to carry out our actions with integrity.

Integrity:

We are servant leaders. We are here to serve. If the opportunity to serve isn't there we wouldn't be in business. Our customers are a platform to serve our purpose. Therefore, we give them importance.

Safety:

To do something, we have to be present. Safety promotes positivity which begets efficiency. Be it patient safety by maintaining high standards of Quality, environmental safety by properly adhering to it.
